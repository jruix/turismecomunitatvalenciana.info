AdSense WordPress Plugin provides a quick an easy way for you to create and manage AdSense across your entire Wordpress site.

== Description ==

This plugin requires you to register your Name and Email Address. Your Name and Email address will be sent to AdSenseWordpressPlugin.com and then the data will be sent to our Aweber subscription list. You can view the terms at our Privacy Policy Page here: http://www.adsensewordpressplugin.com/privacy and the Aweber Privacy Policy here: http://www.aweber.com/privacy.htm. If you have already registered the plugin, simply enter your name and email again and the plugin will be activated immediately. You will not be added to the Aweber list again.

This WordPress plugin for Adsense allows you to easily add Google Adsense into your WordPress blog posts and pages without having to manually code it themselves. This WordPress Adsense plugin incorporates the following features:

1. Easily embed Adsense across all your blog posts or pages just by adding your Adsense Publisher ID.
2. Easily select from all the Adsense sizes and formats to include within your blog.
3. Easily choose the location on your posts or pages where Adsense appears.
4. Easily create a Privacy Policy Page that is AdSense Compliant.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Upload the adsense-wordpress-plugin folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to the AdSense WordPress Plugin Menu and add your AdSense Publisher ID and options.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I add AdSense to all of my pages and posts automatically? =

Yes, the plugin allows you to specify the number of Ads per Page or Per Post. It also allows you to selectively add them to the Home, Category and Archive Pages of your site.

If you don't want ads on all of your pages or posts you can just select 0 Ads per post/page and then use the automatically generated shortcode in order to place the ad units selectively in your pages and posts.

= Can I change the look and feel of my ads? =

Yes, the AdSense WordPress Plugin allows you to full customize the look and feel of your ads within Googles boundaries.
